About Us

Here we go! We are a couple and since we started our relationship, we’ve shared a passion for plants, interior design and travel. We love going around and discover all the nuances of nature, follow the lights of sunset and sunrise and everything there is behind the planet and sustainability!

Who is Roberta?

Roberta (but everyone calls her Rebecca) – 24 y.o. from Bari, lives in London for about a year. She worked for a few years in e-commerce of design products. "Since I moved to London, I started to spend my time around flower markets and botanical coffees. In a chaotic city like London, where the houses are small, the weather often grey and rainy, the plants make me feel in a fresh and colourful world."

Who is Ciccio?

Ciccio – ʧ-ɪ-ʧ-ɪ-ɒ – (here the phonetic if you are finding difficult to pronounce it) - 25 y.o. Sicilian who lives in London for about 6 years and loves plants. How all of this started? After a trip to Amsterdam, he was amazed by those plants what could see behind the big windows of the houses. Especially by one. A plant with holes and huge leaves, what after some research, found out was called “Swiss cheese monstera”. After arriving back to London, he went to the garden close to his house to look for the plant, but could not find it. But came back with 4 plants anyway. So now you can see, how he started to become "plants-addicted".
